Clubs & Teams

Enver creek clubs and teams can be found here. These teams are safe environments that push you to be the best that you can be. If you are not interested in any of these teams or clubs, why not try and make a suggestion or start your own?

Kabadi

Enver Creek has a seasonal kabadi team consisting of 11 people. Enver Creek currently only has a single team due to lack of players. The Kabadi team is always looking for new players!

Robotics

Enver Creek's Robotics team has become very prominent in the world of VEX tournements. The current Robotics team is not living up to their standards as they have no funding. The Robotics team is always looking for new players!

Soccer

Enver Creek's Basketball team has held tryouts not to long ago. With new players entering and going, Enver Creek has multiple teams which include a junior and senior team. Any student is welcome to tryout!
